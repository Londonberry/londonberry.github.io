# Business Goals & Objectives

> Strategic goals and measurable objectives for Londonberry Conversational AI Widget

**Document:** Trigger Map - Business Goals
**Created:** 2026-04-06
**Status:** COMPLETE

---

## Vision

**Transform londonberry.com from a static brochure into a 24/7 conversational experience that connects with every visitor on a human level — qualifying leads through authentic relationship-building, not forms.**

---

## Business Objectives

### ⭐ PRIMARY GOAL: Generate Qualified Leads Through Authentic Conversation (THE ENGINE)

- **Statement:** Convert website visitors into qualified leads by delivering value first and qualifying through emotional resonance
- **Metric:** Qualified leads per month (conversations that reach budget/timeline discussion or calendar booking)
- **Target:** 10 qualified leads per month within 3 months of launch
- **Timeline:** 3 months post-launch
- **Impact:** This drives ALL other objectives — qualified leads become clients, clients become revenue, revenue funds growth

**Objective 1.1: Visitor-to-Conversation Rate**
- **Statement:** Get visitors to click "Start a conversation" and engage meaningfully
- **Metric:** Percentage of unique visitors who initiate and sustain conversation (3+ exchanges)
- **Target:** 15% of unique visitors engage in conversation
- **Timeline:** 3 months post-launch

**Objective 1.2: Conversation-to-Qualified-Lead Rate**
- **Statement:** Convert engaged conversations into qualified leads (budget signal + timeline + clear project idea)
- **Metric:** Percentage of conversations that reach qualification signals
- **Target:** 20% of conversations produce a qualified lead
- **Timeline:** 3 months post-launch

**Objective 1.3: Lead-to-Meeting Rate**
- **Statement:** Move qualified leads to a booked call with Ben
- **Metric:** Percentage of qualified leads who book a call
- **Target:** 50% of qualified leads book a call
- **Timeline:** 3 months post-launch

---

### 🚀 SECONDARY GOALS: Build Trust & Brand Differentiation (Driven by Lead Generation)

**Objective 2.1: Value Delivery Per Visitor**
- **Statement:** Every visitor who engages receives tangible value (prototype image, research, insight) regardless of buy intent
- **Metric:** Percentage of conversations where value artifact is delivered
- **Target:** 80% of conversations include a value delivery moment
- **Timeline:** From launch

**Objective 2.2: Brand Memorability**
- **Statement:** Visitors remember Londonberry as the company that gave them something before asking for anything
- **Metric:** Return visitor rate among conversation participants
- **Target:** 25% of conversation participants return within 60 days
- **Timeline:** 6 months post-launch

**Objective 2.3: Authentic Voice Consistency**
- **Statement:** The AI consistently represents Ben's actual voice — casual, vulnerable, direct
- **Metric:** Conversation sentiment analysis + user feedback
- **Target:** 90% of conversations rated as "authentic/human-feeling" in post-chat survey
- **Timeline:** From launch (iterate continuously)

---

### 🌟 TERTIARY GOALS: Operational Intelligence (Benefits for the Business)

**Objective 3.1: Rich Lead Context**
- **Statement:** Every lead comes with deep context — idea summary, visitor type, engagement signals, budget indicators — not just name and email
- **Metric:** Average data points per lead vs. traditional contact form
- **Target:** 10x more context per lead than a form submission
- **Timeline:** From launch

**Objective 3.2: Market Intelligence**
- **Statement:** Conversations reveal what potential clients are thinking about, struggling with, and willing to pay for
- **Metric:** Monthly insight reports from conversation data
- **Target:** Actionable market insights extracted monthly
- **Timeline:** 3 months post-launch

**Objective 3.3: Reduce Manual Qualification Time**
- **Statement:** Ben spends less time on initial qualification calls because the AI has already done the groundwork
- **Metric:** Time from first contact to proposal
- **Target:** 50% reduction in pre-proposal qualification time
- **Timeline:** 3 months post-launch

---

## The Flywheel: How Goals Connect

**THE ENGINE (Priority #1):**
- Qualified leads through authentic conversation are THE PRIMARY GOAL
- Timeline: 3 months post-launch
- The AI connects with visitors, delivers value, and surfaces qualified prospects
- These qualified leads drive revenue which funds everything else

**Trust & Differentiation (Priority #2):**
- Driven BY the value-first conversation approach
- Every visitor gets something — prototype images, research, memorable insights
- Return visitors and word-of-mouth compound over time
- Timeline: 6 months for measurable brand impact

**Operational Intelligence (Priority #3):**
- Rich conversation data replaces shallow form submissions
- Market insights emerge from aggregate conversation patterns
- Ben's time is leveraged — AI handles initial qualification
- Timeline: 3 months for data pipeline, ongoing refinement
